# Student Study Planner & Exam Countdown

A sleek, dark blue & black themed web application to help students organize their studies and track upcoming exams!

## Features
- ⏱️ **Exam Countdown**: Automatically tracks your next closest exam and displays a live countdown timer (Days, Hours, Minutes).
- ✅ **Task Planner**: Add, complete, and delete your daily study tasks.
- 📚 **Subject Progress**: Visually track how much of a subject you've completed.
- 🗓️ **Exam Manager**: Add all your upcoming exams with specific dates and times.
- 💾 **Local Storage**: Your tasks and exams are saved securely in your browser so you never lose them.
- 🎨 **Dark Theme**: A beautiful dark blue & black UI designed to be easy on the eyes during late-night study sessions.

## How to Use
Open `index.html` in your web browser. 
- Use the **+** buttons on the Tasks or Exams sections to add new items.
- The Countdown Hero section will automatically detect the closest upcoming exam and start ticking!

## Technologies Used
- HTML5
- CSS3 (CSS Variables, Flexbox, Grid, Glassmorphism, Animations)
- Vanilla JavaScript (LocalStorage API, Date/Time calculations)
- Phosphor Icons
