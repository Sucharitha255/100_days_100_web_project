# 🧘 Zen-Gochi

Zen-Gochi is a unique minimalist idle web experience where the objective is to **do absolutely nothing**. Unlike traditional virtual pet games, Zen-Gochi rewards patience and stillness. The longer you avoid unnecessary interactions, the higher your Zen Level grows and the creature continues to evolve.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🧘 Minimalist idle gameplay
- 🌱 Evolution system based on stillness
- 📈 Live Stability meter
- ⭐ Zen Level progression
- 🌌 Animated visual effects
- 💻 Fully responsive user interface
- ⚡ Lightweight and fast with no external dependencies

## ▶️ Usage

- Launch the application.
- Observe the Zen-Gochi creature.
- Avoid unnecessary interactions.
- Maintain stability to increase your Zen Level.
- Watch the creature evolve through different stages.
