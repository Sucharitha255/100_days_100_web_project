Fit Tips

01

Measure in the evening

Feet swell throughout the day. Measuring in the evening gives the most accurate result.

02

Wear your usual socks

Try your shoes on with the socks you'll normally wear for the best fit assessment.

03

Check width

If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up. A snug fit in width means a half-size up.

04

Thumb's width

Leave about a thumb's width of space between your longest toe and the end of the shoe.
