# Color Jump Game

A fast-paced, minimalist jumping game with a modern UI, responsive controls, and comprehensive statistics tracking.



## Features
- **Responsive Design:** Playable on Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile.
- **Touch & Keyboard Controls:** Tap the screen, click the mouse, or press the `Spacebar` to jump.
- **Progressive Difficulty:** Choose from 3 difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard) – obstacles speed up as your score increases.
- **Persistent Storage:** High scores and all game statistics are saved using `localStorage`.
- **Statistics Dashboard:** Collapsible panel showing detailed stats!
  - Total Games Played
  - Total Jumps
  - Highest Score
  - Average Score
  - Current Difficulty
  - Longest Survival Time
  - Total Play Time
  - Best Streak
  - Success Rate (%)
- **Progress Bars:** Visual progress tracking of High Score, Average Score, and Survival Time!
- **Reset Confirmation Dialog:** Confirm before resetting all saved stats (closes on cancel or reset, click outside, or press Escape!
- **Modern UI:** Built with sleek glassmorphism design, glassy gradients, smooth animations, and glowing elements!

## Tech Stack
- HTML5 Canvas
- CSS3 (Variables, Flexbox, Grid, Animations)
- Vanilla JavaScript

## How to Play
1. Choose your difficulty level (Easy/Medium/Hard)
2. Click **Start Game**.
3. Tap the screen or press the Spacebar to keep the glowing square in the air.
4. Dodge the colored blocks coming from the right.
5. Try to beat your high score and track your progress in the Statistics Dashboard!
