How to Play 2048

🏆 Goal

Slide the tiles on the grid. When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one. Reach the 2048 tile to win!

🕹️ Controls

⌨️ Keyboard

↑
← ↓ →

Arrow Keys or WASD

📱 Mobile

Swipe in any direction

💡 Pro Tips

  • Keep your highest tile in a corner.
  • Build a snake chain of descending values.
  • Use ↩ Undo to recover from a bad move.
  • Try 💡 Hint when you are stuck!

⚙️ Game Modes

🎯 Classic 4×4 grid, no time limit
⏱️ Timed 60s Race against the clock
🧘 Zen 5×5 Bigger grid, relaxed play
