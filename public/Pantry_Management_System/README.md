# Food Expiry & Pantry Management System

A beautiful, smart web application designed to help you manage your kitchen inventory, track food expiry dates, and reduce food waste!

## Features
- 🍎 **Add Grocery Items**: Log items with their category, quantity, and expiry date.
- ⏳ **Smart Expiry Tracker**: Items are color-coded based on their expiry date:
  - 🟢 **Green**: Good to eat.
  - 🟡 **Orange**: Expiring within 3 days!
  - 🔴 **Red (Pulsing)**: Expired!
- 🔍 **Search & Filters**: Instantly search your pantry and filter by categories (Produce, Dairy, Meat, Pantry, Frozen, etc.).
- 📶 **Sorting**: Sort your items by Expiry Date or Alphabetically.
- 💾 **Local Storage**: All data is saved directly to your browser—no login required!
- 🎨 **Glassmorphism UI**: A stunning, modern, and responsive user interface with animated backgrounds.

## How to Use
Simply open `index.html` in your web browser. 

Click **Add Item** to log a new grocery item. You can then search, filter, or edit items directly from the beautiful dashboard!

## Technologies Used
- HTML5
- CSS3 (Glassmorphism, Flexbox, Grid, Animations, Variables)
- Vanilla JavaScript (DOM manipulation, LocalStorage API, Date Math)
- Phosphor Icons
