How to Play

  • Goal: Beat the dealer by getting closer to 21 without going over.
  • Hit: Draw another card.
  • Stand: End your turn; dealer draws to 17+.
  • Double Down: Double your bet, draw exactly one card, then stand.
  • Split: When your first two cards share a rank, split into two hands. Each hand plays independently with the same bet.
  • Bust: Going over 21 is an automatic loss.
  • Blackjack: Ace + 10-value card on the deal pays 1.5× bet.
  • Aces count as 1 or 11, whichever helps more.
  • Face cards (K, Q, J) are worth 10.
