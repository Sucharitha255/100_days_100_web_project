How can we help you?

Find answers to common questions, navigation tips, and contact information.

How do I get turn-by-turn navigation?
You can use the search bar on the Home page to select your destination, or click on any location pin on the Map view. Click the "Navigate" button to generate the optimal route.
Does navigation work offline?
RouteMaster requires an internet connection for real-time occupancy updates, but base maps and pre-calculated routes will remain visible if you lose connection.
Are accessible routes available?
Yes! You can toggle accessibility settings in the route options to prioritize ramps and elevators instead of stairs across all buildings.
I found a closed path or wrong location.
Campus layouts change. If you spot a locked door, construction zone, or missing room, please report it to us so we can update the live map.

Still need help?

Reach out to the university support desk or map administration.
