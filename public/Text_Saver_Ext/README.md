# 🚀 Text Saver Pro

A premium Chrome Extension designed for researchers, students, and professionals to capture, organize, and reference information from the web with unparalleled ease.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🛠 Powerful Capture
- **One-Key Save**: Just press **'s'** to instantly save your selected text.
- **Smart Double-Click**: Double-click any paragraph to save it immediately.
- **Context Menu**: Right-click any selection and choose "Save to Text Saver."

### 📂 Advanced Organization (Pro)
- **Categories & Tagging**: Add custom tags to your snippets to keep projects organized.
- **Smart Search**: Filter through hundreds of notes by content, URL, or tag in real-time.
- **Individual Management**: Delete or edit specific snippets without losing your entire history.

### ✍️ Research Tools (Pro)
- **Notes Feature**: Add your own comments or "to-dos" directly to any saved snippet.
- **Citation Generator**: Instantly generate APA-style citations for your research papers.
- **Text Fragment Highlighting**: Click a saved link to open the page and have Chrome **automatically scroll to and highlight** your captured text.

### 📤 Export & Portability
- **Modern UI**: A beautiful Glassmorphism interface with dark-mode elegance.
- **Toast Notifications**: Get instant visual confirmation on-page when text is saved.
- **Clean Exports**: Download your organized research as a formatted `.txt` file.

## 🚀 Installation

1.  Clone or download this repository.
2.  Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3.  Enable **Developer mode** in the top-right corner.
4.  Click **Load unpacked** and select the `Text_Saver_Ext` folder.
5.  Pin the extension to your toolbar for easy access!

## 🧪 Usage Tips
- **Avoid Conflicts**: The 's' shortcut is disabled while you are typing in forms or text boxes.
- **Privacy**: All your data is stored locally in your browser—no external servers are involved.
