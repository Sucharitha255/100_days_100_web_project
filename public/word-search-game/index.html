How To Play

Find all the hidden words listed in the panel to complete the puzzle!

Select Words: Click/touch and drag across letters to connect them. You can select horizontal, vertical, or diagonal lines.
Directions: Word directions depend on difficulty:
  • Easy: Forward horizontal and vertical only.
  • Medium: Forward horizontal, vertical, and diagonal.
  • Hard/Expert: Horizontal, vertical, diagonal—both forward and backward.
Hints: Starcharts running low? Use the Hint button to briefly highlight the first letter of a random remaining word (costs score).
Daily Challenge: Play a unique, hand-crafted daily word search grid shared with players around the globe!
