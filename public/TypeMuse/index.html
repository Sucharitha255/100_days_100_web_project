About Typography

Typography is the art of giving words personality, structure, and emotional tone.

Great typography balances contrast, hierarchy, spacing, and rhythm. A bold heading grabs attention, while a calm body font makes reading effortless. Pairing fonts is about creating harmony between different voices.

Use generous line-height, readable sizes, consistent spacing, and intentional contrast. Tiny details like letter spacing, weight, and animation timing can turn a simple layout into a memorable brand experience.
